Privacy Policy – TellTrack

Last updated: 28 March 2026

TellTrack ("the app", "we") is a GPS trail navigation app that records your movement and guides you back along your route. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

Location data

The app continuously records your GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude, and timestamp) while a recording session is active. This happens in the foreground and in the background (while your screen is locked or the app is not visible) so that your track is not lost during a ski run or hike. Location data is stored locally on your device in GPX format.

Recorded tracks (uploaded to our server)

When a recording ends, the app uploads your GPX track to our server at track.shippy.no. The upload includes:

  • The GPX file (timestamped GPS coordinates for the recorded session)
  • An anonymous device identifier (Apple's identifierForVendor — resets if you uninstall the app and cannot be used to identify you across apps or companies)
  • Metadata: number of GPS points, distance (metres), and duration
  • A randomly generated UUID for the track (prevents duplicate uploads)

If your device is offline when a session ends, the upload is queued and retried automatically when connectivity is restored.

GPX files you open

The app can open .gpx files from other apps or your Files app. These files are read locally and are never uploaded.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking
  • Crash logs or analytics beyond what Apple provides to all developers

3. How We Use the Data

Uploaded tracks are used solely to:

  • Build and improve the trail database that powers in-app navigation
  • Detect and correct errors in existing trail routes

Track data is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose unrelated to trail navigation.

4. Data Retention

Tracks are stored on our server for as long as they are useful for trail improvement. You may request deletion of any track associated with your device identifier by contacting us (see Section 7).

5. Legal Basis (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing location data is legitimate interest (improving trail navigation for the app's users). No personal data as defined by GDPR (name, email, etc.) is collected or transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app and you have concerns, please contact us.
