Privacy Policy – TellTrack

Last updated: 29 March 2026

TellTrack ("the app") is a navigation app that records your movement and guides you back along your route. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data that are collected

Location data

The app continuously records your location (latitude, longitude, altitude, and timestamp) while a recording session is active. This happens in the foreground and in the background (while your screen is locked or the app is not visible) so that your track is not lost during a skiing trip/sailing trip or hike. Location data is stored locally on your device.

Recorded tracks

When a recording ends, the app uploads your track. The upload includes:

  • The GPX file (timestamped GPS coordinates for the recorded session)
  • An anonymous device identifier (Apple's identifierForVendor — resets if you uninstall the app and cannot be used to identify you across apps or companies)
  • Metadata: number of GPS points, distance (metres), and duration
  • A randomly generated UUID for the track (prevents duplicate uploads)

If your device is offline when a session ends, the upload is queued and retried automatically when connectivity is restored.

2. Data that are NOT collected

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking
  • Crash logs or analytics beyond what Apple provides to all developers

3. How the Data is used

Uploaded tracks are used solely to:

  • Improve the app to make a better experience for the users
  • Detect and correct errors in the app
  • Statistics on number of users, usage and world coverage

Track data is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any other purpose.

4. Data Retention

Tracks may be stored for as long as they are useful for improvements and statistics.

5. Legal Basis (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing location data is legitimate interest (improving trail navigation for the app's users). No personal data as defined by GDPR (name, email, etc.) is collected or transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
